Portland is the largest city in the State of Oregon. It was named after the City of Portland, Maine during a coin toss between the city's two founders. Portland boasts an eclectic culture and a city slogan of "Keep Portland Weird"! Portland is often considered the most bike friendly city in America and is also one of the fastest growing cities in the nation.
