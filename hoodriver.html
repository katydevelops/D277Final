HOOD RIVER

The city of Hood River is home to 31,000 acres of scenic national Oregon forest!

    Facts about Hood River:
  • City's Population: 8,341
  • Year Incorporated: July 28, 1933
  • Region of State: Located at top most north central area of Oregon, located at border of Washington
  • City Classification: Rural
  • Average Family Income of Hood River: $97,57
  • Average Income Compared to State: Hood River average income higher than Oregon state

The city of Hood River is a rural area that sits on the Columbia, River. Due to it's scenic location, the area is best known for it's outdoor activities which include hiking, mountain biking, boating, wind surfing and mushroom hunting. There are numerous large waterfalls in Hood River which include the Punch Bowl Falls, the Wahclella Falls and the Latourell Falls. Many of the Hood River hikes give travelers a majestic view of Mt. Hood in nearby Portland.
